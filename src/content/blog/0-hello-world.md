---
title: "So, a blog?"
description: "Why I decided to start a blog and what I hope to achieve with it."
date: 2026-07-21
---

Beyond my research area, there are many things I am passionate about. There are also many things that I have spent countless hours pondering over yet never really had the right outlet to express.
Things like philosophy, theology, socio-, psycho-, and historical linguistics, among other topics, would (in all likelihood) not make it to my publication list any time soon. However, I would rather that this blog be the place where I can 
share my thoughts on these topics, in case there is any curious soul out there who wants to read it.
